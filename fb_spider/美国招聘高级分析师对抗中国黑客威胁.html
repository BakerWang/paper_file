美国招聘高级分析师对抗中国黑客威胁

大伯伯 2013-03-29 共117479人围观 ，发现 28 个不明物体 资讯

下图为美国HBGary公司的招聘页

翻译:

我们寻找能协助我们分析跟踪来自中国网络攻击的高级分析师.

你必须要能从收集到的信息里分析其中的网络威胁,分析这些威胁的来源,他们的目的和他们的行为.
其中一个最主要的职责就是跟踪中国雇佣黑客对美国国防部,国防承包商的间谍活动

我们要求你必须是中国黑客圈子的活跃人士,而且对中国雇佣黑客这个事有一个直接的认识(言下之意就是你最好在相关组织待过.反水人士最欢迎)

我们希望你能持续的报告中国黑客的一般攻击手法 他们使用的工具 漏洞利用程序 远控木马等

最后,关于工作地点的选择 

1.最好能合法的去美国加利福尼亚
2.在天朝卧底也行,我们可以用visa给你付工资.

原文:

HBGary seeks a senior analyst to assist in identifying and tracking cyber-threats originating primarily from the country of China. The threat analyst plays an integral role in this mission by serving as an expert resource in the collection, analysis, and presentation of data surrounding active, operating cyber threats. The analyst will characterize hacker threats based on origin, intent, and operating behaviors. A key responsibility will be tracking relationships between the Chinese state-owned agencies and Chinese military, and an ongoing campaign of industrial espionage activity being carried out against the US Department of Defense and associated defense contractors and manufacturing bases. The candidate should have a fluent and established relationship with the hacking subculture of China, and have direct existing knowledge of state-sponsored hacking activity. The analyst will be expected to continue his or her relationship with the hacking subculture of China and remain abreast of developing attack vectors, exploits, and remote-access tools. Experience with malware command-and-control schemes is considered a bonus. Candidate must be able to work legally in the United States. Ideally, this analyst will be located in HBGary’s U.S. California office. Relocation and work visa sponsorhsip is available.
